CHAdeMO Adapter

Enjoy the most charging options of any EV owner on the road with the CHAdeMO adapter.

Compatible with third-party DC fast charging networks such as Electrify America and EVgo, the CHAdeMO adapter supports charging speeds up to 50kW and works with all Tesla vehicles (2019.24.1 firmware or later is required for Model 3 compatibility).

Note: Tesla does not regulate pricing or charging experience at third-party charging stations.

Network membership is typically required at third-party CHAdeMO stations. For more details on non-Tesla charging practices, please consult third-party EVSEs directly.

Compatible with Model S, Model 3, Model X and Model Y.
